Welcome to Tender Vine Church Global

Tender Vine Church Global is a church that depend solely on the word of God and Prayer. We help people grow spiritually through our daily service and monthly teaching series. Interceding for others is our normal lifestyle, helping other people grow is what we are called to do. We believe in fellowshipping with the saints of God, the scripture also said we should'nt forsake the assembly of one another. Join our community let's help you understand the importance of fellowship and also studying the word.

We are Word and Prayer Addict!!

Vision & Mission statement

Rebuilding the Tebernacle of David

Tebernacle of David is a dwelling place that David made for the presence of God in the city of David. (see psalms 132:1-5, 6-18.) when God said i will rebuild the tebernacle of David it means that the people that will fellowship with Him will enjoy the flow of His presence and that is what we are called to do.

Head Pastor: Paul Aremo-Oluwa

Paul Aremo-Oluwa is called by God as an Apostle graced with prophetic insights. He speaks to the Church, Nigeria and other nations. He is a Songwriter, teacher of God's word, the Head Pastor at Tender Vine Church Global (TVCG) and the convener of Tender Vine Ministries (TVM) with a strong emphasis on communicating the gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ with simplicity. He has published several articles and mini books along with different podcast teaching series available on Anchor Fm and Telegram. He is married and based in Nigeria with his family.
